Baseline

We trained nano4M for half the original schedule (~2h, 15,259 steps), stopping once we hit our target validation loss of 3.8.

Evaluation Protocol

Each extension is measured on four axes:

  1. Wall-clock time — total training duration
  2. Steps to loss 3.8 — potential training time = (s / 15,259) × t
  3. Throughput — tokens/sec and MFU
  4. Downstream quality — Depth, Normals, Text, RGB + visual inspection
